# Target House 3 Assumptions

- `target-house-2.png` is the primary visual target, with `floorplan.png` used for the 14 m x 10 m programme and room proportions.
- The folded white wrapper is represented with typed upper walls, an asymmetric white roof, recess zones, return walls, railings, and terrace floor geometry rather than a final mass placeholder.
- The right loggia bay is modelled as full-height glazing instead of an operable door so the visible facade remains faithful while avoiding the target-house-2 door-operation conflict at the roof-court return walls.
- Interior assets are schematic programme markers sized from the indexed asset library. They were shifted inward from walls, doors, windows, and each other to keep collision and operation-clearance Advisor findings at zero.
- Browser screenshot evidence is unavailable in this run because the shared web app currently fails to compile before the app shell loads.
