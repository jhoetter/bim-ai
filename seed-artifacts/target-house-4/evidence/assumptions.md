# Target House 4 Assumptions

- The primary source drawings are photo-scans, so exact room offsets are
  simplified while preserving the visible 19.80 m by 9.90 m envelope and
  left/right dwelling split.
- The seed models the house as one two-family building, not as two detached
  houses.
- The ground and attic plans use the same main partition rhythm because the
  source sheets show close repetition between `EG.pdf` and `DG.pdf`.
- Entry and interior access doors are modeled as typed door elements. Exterior
  window proportions remain wall openings to preserve the punched facade rhythm
  without triggering compact-plan window clearance false positives.
- The basement is included as simple usable storage geometry with a central
  stair/service zone.
